# Ergonomic Desktop Surface — Prototype Checklist

## Dimensions
- Size: 140 × 75 cm (1400 × 750 mm)

## Ergonomics
- [ ] Verify the desktop is ergonomic in the standing position, not just seated.

## Materials & shopping
- [ ] Buy MDF 25mm 1500x750 panel sheet.
- [ ] Hardwood strip (~25 × 50 mm) glued + screwed under the back edge to spread monitor-arm clamp load and protect the MDF. Laminate flooring leftovers work.
